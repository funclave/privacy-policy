1. Privacy Policy

Funclave ("we," "our," or "the Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Information Collection and Use

  • Automatically Collected Data: IP address, device model, OS version, access logs, and service usage patterns (used for service optimization).
  • User-Provided Data: Email address (collected only for customer support and inquiry handling).

Third-Party Services (Updated)

To provide and improve our services, we integrate third-party SDKs. These providers may collect and process data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

1. Advertising

  • AdMob, Unity Ads, AppLovin: Used to display personalized or non-personalized advertisements within the app.

2. Analytics

  • Google Analytics for Firebase, Unity Analytics: Used to analyze user behavior, app performance, and improve service quality.

3. Authentication and Game Services

  • Google Play Games Services, Apple ID: Used for user authentication, cloud saves, leaderboards, and achievement tracking.

For more details on how these providers handle your data, please refer to their respective official privacy policy pages.

Data Retention and Deletion

  • Personal information is promptly deleted once the purpose of collection has been achieved.
  • Digital files are permanently erased using technical methods that prevent any record recovery.

Protection of Minors

  • We do not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 14 (or the age required by local law). If we discover such data has been collected, it will be deleted immediately.

Contact Information (Privacy Officer)

For any privacy-related inquiries, please contact our dedicated team:

  • Email: cs@funclave.net
